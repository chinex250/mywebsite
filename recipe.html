CAKE RECIPE

Ingrendients

  • Butter/margerine
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Milk{optional}
  • Vanilla essence
  • Baking powder

Procedure

  1. Mix the butter and the sugar until its fluffy
  2. Whisk the egg and add it into the batter and mix very well
  3. Sieve in all the powdered ingrendients and mix very well until a smooth and fluffy batter is formed
  4. Get your baking pan and rub in butter to prevent the cake from sticking to the pan
  5. After that,pour the cake mixture into the pan and bake
  6. After about 45-60 mins check the cake by deeping a clean toothpick into middle of the cake,if the cake is done the toothpick will come out clean.
  7. Ice your cake the way you want and enjoy
